Why GameNative?

Safe Steam Login — Your credentials never leave your device. We don't store or transmit them.
No Cloud Required — GameNative runs games using your Android's own hardware. No external PC or stream server needed.
Built on Pluvia & Winlator — Powered by open-source tech, improved for usability and performance.
